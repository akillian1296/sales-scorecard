Connect to HubSpot

This scorecard pulls live activity data directly from your HubSpot portal. You need a Private App token and your Portal ID. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

  1. 1
    In HubSpot, go to Settings → Integrations → Private Apps and click Create a private app.
  2. 2
    Give it a name (e.g. Sales Scorecard). Under Scopes, enable: crm.objects.contacts.read, crm.objects.engagements.read, crm.objects.owners.read.
  3. 3
    Click Create app and copy the token shown. It's only shown once — paste it below immediately.
  4. 4
    Find your Portal ID: it's the number in your HubSpot URL — app.hubspot.com/contacts/12345678/.
  5. 5
    Paste both below and click Save & Connect. The scorecard will test the connection and fetch your data.
HubSpot Private App Token

The internal name of your custom contact property for fleet/vehicle count. Find it in HubSpot → Settings → Properties → Contact properties. Use the internal name (e.g. fleet_size), not the label.

How scores are calculated

Meeting with 10+ vehicles (fleet_size ≥ 10) 1.0 pt
Meeting with <10 vehicles (fleet_size < 10) 0.5 pt
Calls logged (pro-rated per 25) calls ÷ 25
Second meeting (2nd+ meeting with same contact) 0.5 pt
