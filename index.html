Activity scoring rules
Sales reps (auto-populated from HubSpot owners)
Reps are pulled automatically from HubSpot owners. Toggle Active/Inactive to control who appears on the scorecard. New reps pulled from HubSpot default to Inactive — toggle them on when they join the team.

Connect to HubSpot

Enter your HubSpot Private App token and Portal ID. The scorecard will pull calls and meetings directly from your account.

  1. 1
    In HubSpot go to Settings → Integrations → Private Apps → Create a private app.
  2. 2
    Enable these scopes: crm.objects.contacts.read, crm.objects.owners.read, sales-email-read, timeline.
  3. 3
    Click Create app, copy the token, and paste it below.
  4. 4
    Your Portal ID is the number in your HubSpot URL: app.hubspot.com/contacts/12345678/
Private App Token
Saved in your browser only. Never transmitted anywhere except directly to HubSpot.

The internal name of your contact property for vehicle/fleet count. Found in HubSpot → Settings → Properties → Contact properties. Click the property and look for the internal name field.
