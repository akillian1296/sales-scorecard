Import from HubSpot

Export your activity data from HubSpot and drop it below. All processing happens in your browser — no data is sent anywhere.

  1. 1
    In HubSpot go to Reports → Activities or use Sales → Sales Activity Report.
  2. 2
    Filter by your desired date range. Make sure these columns are included: Activity assigned to, Activity date, Activity type, and your fleet size property (e.g. Number of vehicles).
  3. 3
    Click Export → CSV and save the file.
  4. 4
    Drop the CSV below. Map your columns, then click Import. Scores update instantly.
